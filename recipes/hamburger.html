Hamburger

Description

The best basic burger in the universe

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • ½ cup fine dry bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Preheat grill
  2. Whisk together egg, salt, and pepper in a bowl
  3. Add ground beef and bread crumbs and mix
  4. Form into four 3/4-inch-thick patties
  5. Place patties on the preheated grill. Cover and cook
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
